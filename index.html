Flagship product

A deterministic QA trust layer for the AI-coding era. Every finding comes with proof.

As AI writes more of your code, the hard part shifts from writing it to trusting it. RRK Verify scans your repo, scores its testability, and shows the exact evidence behind every finding, so your team can act without guessing. Your source stays on your machine.

01

Scan

A read-only scan flags fragile selectors, missing and duplicate test-ids, and coverage gaps. Same input, same result.

02

Map

A testability score and ranked fixes, built from a deterministic rule pack with no model in the decision path.

03

Evidence

Every finding cites file, line, and a copyable locator. "Unclear" is a valid answer, never a false pass.

04

Verify

Record a real flow, generate a Playwright draft, run it, and only on your approval write the test into your repo.
