RRK Verify

QA intelligence for modern software teams and AI agents.

RRK Verify scans software projects, maps testability risks, detects fragile selectors, understands test layers, and gives teams evidence-based recommendations before AI writes or changes tests.

What it does

  • Maps testability risk across a project
  • Detects fragile selectors before they break
  • Understands test layers — unit, API, UI, data
  • Evidence-based recommendations before AI writes or changes tests
